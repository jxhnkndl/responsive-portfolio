About Me

Hiya! My name is J.K. Royston and I love to build things.

I'm a former touring musician, music producer, and songwriter turned full-stack web developer. Whether it's a simple landing page, a complete full-stack web application, or a commercial quality audio product, my goal is to create beautiful digital experiences designed to captivate, inform, and call your target audience to action.

I started my development journey at the beginning of 2020 using Codecademy's interactive platform and video courses on Udemy. After 8 months of self-paced study, I enrolled in the University of Richmond's Coding Bootcamp and will be graduating in May 2021 having gained experience working with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Skeleton CSS, responsive web design, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, SQL, MongoDB, and React.js. I'm interested in full stack development, web design, UX design, and audio technology. You can usually find me working on an application or producing a record for an up-and-coming alternative pop artist.

Outside of development, I'm a former touring musician, an avid record collector, a die-hard hockey fan, and dad to a pretty awesome rescue pup.
